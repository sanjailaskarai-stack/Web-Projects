Visit Japan

Japan is a captivating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge innovation.

Anime

Anime (Japanese: アニメ) is a distinctive style of animation originating from Japan that has evolved into a global cultural phenomenon.

Food

Japanese cuisine, or washoku, is a world-renowned culinary tradition celebrated for its emphasis on seasonality, fresh ingredients, and aesthetic presentation.

Temples

Shinto shrines (神社, jinja) are sacred spaces in Japan dedicated to the worship of kami, or divine spirits believed to inhabit nature, objects, and ancestors.
